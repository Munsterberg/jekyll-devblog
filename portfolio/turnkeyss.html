Summary: Turnkeyss was a small static site project that required delicate attention from a dedicated developer. I worked with the client over email to create a design strategy and to understand exaclty what they wanted implemented. They expressed the fact they wanted to include a lot of sliders, a contact form, and make use of a google map API.

Technology Used: HTML, SASS, PHP, JavaScript, PhotoShop, and Illustrator.

Length: This project was short and concise. It was developed and deployed in under a week.
